The UK's 5-million-plus small and midsize businesses are increasingly putting AI to work to operate more efficiently and move faster. Google Cloud reports 71% of surveyed UK AI adopters say AI saves time on routine tasks, 64% report a direct productivity boost, Google Workspace with Gemini delivers a 20% productivity boost for SMBs, and UK SMBs using Google Cloud AI have nearly doubled year-over-year.
